Titan Lab Tours

✻

Titan Lab Tour is an event for middle school and high school Girl Scouts where we introduce STEM opportunities and labs offered at CSUF. We take these young women on a tour around our campus to give them exposure to a college campus as well as encourage them to pursue a career in the STEM field.

✻

Tours

The tours offered during the event range from a variety of STEM fields such as the computer science fields, engineering fields, health fields, and more.
